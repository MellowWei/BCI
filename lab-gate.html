THE QUESTION // 核心问题

Non-invasive neural intervention is methodologically unresolved at its core.

非侵入式神经干预在方法论层面仍未解决其核心问题。

How can such an intervention establish causal effects on subjective experience when it is embedded in daily context and cannot be randomized over time? Most current BCI research operates within an engineering frame that treats this question as deferred. This project addresses it directly. Around this question, a building theoretical framework, a falsifiable experimental design, and a prototype intervention system have been constructed. The aim is collaboration that produces a methodologically rigorous validation pathway at the neuroscience–phenomenology boundary.

当干预嵌入日常情境、无法随时间随机化时，它如何证明对主观体验的因果效应？现有 BCI 研究大多在工程框架下运作，将这一问题悬置。本项目直接处理它。围绕这一问题，已经搭建了一个建立中的理论框架、一套可证伪的实验设计、以及一个原型干预系统。目标是促成在神经科学与现象学交界处具有方法论严格性的验证路径合作。

PART 02 // THEORETICAL RATIONALE // 理论依据

Why this research question must be asked now.

为什么现在必须问这个研究问题。

PROBLEM FIELD // 问题场域

A post-pandemic adult attention crisis without non-clinical infrastructure.

缺乏非临床基础设施的后疫情成人注意力危机。

Adult attention and emotional self-regulation has emerged as a defining condition of the post-pandemic environment. Epidemiological data show 49.9% of US adults aged 18–24 reported anxiety or depression symptoms in 2023 (KFF 2023), with attention fragmentation and burnout consistently named as core lived experiences (Ipsos 2023). National data show no return to pre-pandemic mental wellbeing levels (Kiviruusu et al. 2024, Lancet Psychiatry).

Existing response infrastructures presume a clinical pathway. But a substantial portion of this population is non-clinical, neurodivergent-affirming, and resistant to symptom-based framings. They need tools that treat attention and affect as regulable state, not as diagnosable condition.

现有应对基础设施假设临床路径。但这一人群中相当部分是非临床的、神经多样性肯定的、抗拒症状化框架的。他们需要把注意力与情绪视为可调节状态而非可诊断病情的工具。

THEORETICAL ANCHOR // 理论锚点

Phenomenology of qualia · biopolitics of self-regulation · scaffolding without command.

感质现象学 · 自我调节的生命政治 · 不下命令的脚手架。

The framework rests on a phenomenological commitment that subjective state texture — qualia — precedes diagnostic categorization. This draws on Husserl's analysis of internal time-consciousness, Merleau-Ponty's embodied perception, Varela's neurophenomenology, and engages Chalmers' hard problem by designing around it: the system samples first-person state and responds to it.

A second commitment is critical: existing self-regulation tools embed what Foucault termed biopolitical logic — bodily and attentional states rendered as objects of optimization and surveillance. The proposed framework treats AI-mediated feedback as scaffolding in Vygotsky's sense — supporting internalization, not replacing it.

该框架建立在现象学承诺之上：感质先于诊断性归类。批判性承诺：拒绝把现有自我调节工具中的生命政治逻辑。AI 反馈作为维果茨基意义上的脚手架——支持内化，不替代它。

THE SPECIFIC GAP // 具体缺口

No current line of work integrates four components in a single intervention.

目前没有任何研究线整合这四个组件。

Despite extensive parallel research, no current line integrates: (1) adaptive auditory environment that responds to user state in real time, (2) AI-mediated reflective dialogue that scaffolds without commanding, (3) wearable physiological convergence as validation layer, and (4) explicit non-clinical, neurodivergence-affirming framing.

Patel's OPERA hypothesis (2011) demonstrates music drives plasticity — but assumes fixed content. Brain.fm's neural phase-locking research (2024, Nature Communications) elevates attention-associated beta activity by 119% — but is non-adaptive. Self-regulated learning research from Zimmerman through Bodong Chen establishes AI-scaffolded reflection — but is focused on K-12 task contexts. Affective computing (Picard, Empatica) treats state regulation as recognition-and-response rather than temporal pathway.

Three 2024–2026 developments make this the right moment: large language models reach reflective fluency for state-sensitive scaffolding; consumer wearable HRV reaches research-grade reliability; the post-pandemic adult population produces population-scale demand for non-clinical, sovereignty-preserving regulation tools.

尽管在情感计算、功能性音乐、神经同步、自我调节学习存在大量并行研究，目前没有任何研究线整合这四个组件于一个干预：自适应声音环境 + AI 中介反思对话 + 可穿戴生理收敛 + 非诊断神经多样性肯定框架。三个 2024–2026 才成熟的条件让现在成为合适时机：LLM 反思流畅度、消费级 HRV 研究级可靠性、后疫情成人对非临床调节工具的人群级需求。

PART 03 // RESEARCH QUESTIONS // 研究问题

Three research questions. Three hypotheses. Three falsifiability conditions.

三个研究问题。三个假设。三个可证伪条件。

The research is structured as three layered RQs: a main-effect question, a multi-layer convergence question, and a mechanism / moderation question. Each maps to a directional hypothesis with explicit falsifiability conditions.

研究分为三层 RQ：主效应、多层指标收敛、机制 / 调节。每一层对应一个方向性假设和明确的可证伪条件。

RQ1 // MAIN EFFECT // 主效应
RQ1 · RESEARCH QUESTION
Does an adaptive auditory-AI intervention condition produce greater within-session attentional and emotional state recovery than fixed-rhythm, random-rhythm, and silence baseline conditions, in non-clinical adults reporting attention or affective dysregulation?
RQ1 · 研究问题
自适应声音-AI 干预条件，在自我报告存在注意力或情感失调的非临床成人中，是否比固定节律、随机节律和静音基线产生更强的 session 内注意力与情绪状态恢复？

H1 · HYPOTHESIS

Condition D (adaptive auditory environment + AI reflective scaffolding) will produce statistically significant greater pre/post improvement on at least two of three measurement layers — self-report state inventory, behavioral sustained-attention task, physiological convergence (HRV-RMSSD and/or EDA tonic level) — compared to Conditions A (silence), B (fixed rhythm), C (random rhythm), with effect sizes meaningful at Cohen's d > 0.4.

条件 D 将在三层测量中至少两层产生统计学显著改善——自评、行为持续注意任务、生理收敛——优于条件 A/B/C，效应量 Cohen's d > 0.4。

FALSIFIABILITY · 可证伪性

If Condition D does not produce statistically significant improvement over A, B, and C on at least two of three layers, H1 is rejected and the core intervention assumption requires theoretical revision. // 若条件 D 在至少两层未显著优于 A/B/C，H1 被拒绝，干预核心假设需要理论修订。

RQ2 // MULTI-LAYER CONVERGENCE // 多层指标收敛
RQ2 · RESEARCH QUESTION
To what extent do subjective self-report state recovery, behavioral attention task performance, and physiological proxies converge directionally within the adaptive condition?
RQ2 · 研究问题
在自适应条件中，主观自我报告状态恢复、行为注意力任务表现、与生理代理三层指标在方向上的收敛程度如何？

H2 · HYPOTHESIS

In Condition D, subjective state recovery (self-report) will show directional convergence with behavioral measures (sustained-attention task accuracy and reaction time) and at least one physiological measure (HRV-RMSSD or EDA tonic level), with within-subject correlation r > 0.3.

在条件 D 中，主观状态恢复将与行为指标和至少一项生理指标显示方向性收敛，被试内相关系数 r > 0.3。

FALSIFIABILITY · 可证伪性

If self-report improves but behavioral and physiological measures show no directional alignment, H2 is rejected and the intervention may operate through perceived rather than physiologically grounded state change. // 若自我报告改善但行为生理方向不一致，H2 被拒绝，干预可能仅通过主观感知起作用。

RQ3 // MECHANISM / MODERATION // 机制 / 调节
RQ3 · RESEARCH QUESTION
How does the AI reflective scaffolding mode (stabilization vs expansion) moderate the relationship between rhythm condition and state recovery?
RQ3 · 研究问题
AI 反思脚手架模式（稳定 vs 扩展）如何调节节律条件与状态恢复之间的关系？

H3 · HYPOTHESIS

AI scaffolding mode will moderate Condition D's effect: stabilization scaffolding (AiQ愛<3) will produce stronger recovery when pre-session state shows high arousal (high EDA tonic, low HRV-RMSSD); expansion scaffolding (AiQ愛<7) will produce stronger recovery when pre-session state shows low engagement (low arousal + low task readiness).

稳定脚手架在高唤醒前态时产生更强恢复；扩展脚手架在低投入前态时产生更强恢复。

FALSIFIABILITY · 可证伪性

If AI mode × pre-session state interaction is non-significant, H3 is rejected and AI scaffolding design requires theoretical revision. // 若 AI 模式 × 前态交互不显著，H3 被拒绝，AI 脚手架设计需要理论修订。

Not music therapy.

不是音乐疗法。

Music therapy asks whether music reduces clinical symptoms over extended treatment. This asks whether a specific rhythm parameter — adaptive vs. fixed vs. random — produces measurable within-session state change. The outcome is a state variable, not a symptom score. The population is non-clinical.

音乐疗法问的是音乐是否在长期治疗中减少临床症状。HRP 问的是特定节律参数是否在单次 session 内产生可测量的状态变化。结果变量是状态，不是症状分数。人群是非临床的。

Not standard BCI.

不是标准脑机接口。

Standard BCI research decodes neural signals to control external devices. HRP inverts the direction: physiological signals modulate an auditory feedback loop. The system responds to the user's rhythm state; it does not issue commands to the user. The claim is about adaptive feedback efficacy, not neural decoding accuracy.

标准脑机接口研究解码神经信号以控制外部设备。HRP 反转了方向：生理信号调制声音反馈回路。系统响应用户的节律状态，而不是向用户发出命令。

THE CAUSAL INFERENCE PROBLEM // 因果推断问题

Why observational causal methods are the only rigorous path.

为什么观察性因果方法是唯一严格的路径。

The intervention is embedded in daily context. Participants cannot be randomly assigned to receive adaptive rhythm for six months of their life. This means the core validation challenge is methodological: how to distinguish the causal effect of the adaptive condition from regression to the mean, novelty effects, expectation, and concurrent environmental variables. Pre/post correlations are insufficient. Observational causal design — instrumental variables, difference-in-differences, synthetic controls, or interrupted time series — is required. This is precisely the domain where collaboration with a computationally oriented neuroscience lab creates publishable value that neither party could reach alone.

干预嵌入在日常情境中。参与者不能被随机分配去"在接下来六个月里接受自适应节律"。这意味着核心验证挑战是方法论性的：如何把自适应条件的因果效应与均值回归、新奇性效应、预期效应和并发环境变量区分开来。前后测相关性是不够的。需要观察性因果设计。这正是双方合作创造双方单独都无法达到的发表价值的精确领域。

FOUR CONDITIONS // 四个条件
A
Baseline / Silence
Ambient environment or silence. Establishes baseline state trajectory without auditory intervention. Controls for time-on-task and session structure effects.
B
Fixed Rhythm
Constant BPM, fixed genre profile. Tests whether rhythmic structure alone — independent of adaptivity — produces state change relative to baseline.
C
Random Rhythm
Variable BPM and genre, non-adaptive. Controls for novelty, auditory exposure, and expectation effects. Isolates the contribution of adaptivity from variation alone.
D ★
Adaptive Rhythm + AI Feedback
Rhythm parameters adjusted in real-time based on self-report state sampling and behavioral proxies. AI provides reflective micro-dialogue. The core HRP condition under test. Stimulus pool draws from the seven playable genre profiles.

CALIBRATION STATUS · IN PROGRESS // 校准状态 · 进行中

5 protocol calibration runs · all Condition A · pre-IRB · verbal consent only.

5 次协议校准 · 全部 A 条件 · pre-IRB · 仅口头同意。

An informal calibration series is in progress under Condition A (silent baseline, 15 min per session, in-vivo settings). Session S01 (self-experiment, 2026-05-24) produced a near-ceiling recovery score on the 5-axis self-report inventory (mood +2.5, focus +1, body tension reduction +2, recovery 10/10), with approximately six self-initiated countdown checks during the session. Sessions S02–S05 (peer-volunteer informal sessions) are running 2026-05-27 to 2026-05-28.

The purpose is design calibration: to test whether the silent-baseline ceiling effect observed in S01 replicates across peer subjects, which would have direct implications for power-analysis assumptions in the formal four-condition pilot. The formal pilot will require sample-size estimation that accounts for the calibration findings rather than assuming standard regression-to-the-mean parameters.
